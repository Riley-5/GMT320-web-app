Who We Are

We Are GitHubbers

We are a young and talented group of 3 GMT 320 students from the University of Pretoria with a groundbreaking idea designed to get people to understand the significance of having the Hatfield CID and to let everyone know about the CID's contribution towards regulating crime. As GitHubbers, we believe that the CID has the right to know that people appreciate the change that they are bringing to our community as well as for the hard work they are doing.
